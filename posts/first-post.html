How I Outline Without Killing the Fun

I outline just enough to know where I’m going, never so much that I’m bored getting there.

The 5 cards I start with

  1. Hook — what changes in the first 3 pages?
  2. Door — the choice that makes turning back impossible.
  3. Mid problem — what makes the hero’s approach fail?
  4. Mirror — the moment of painful self-recognition.
  5. After — the new normal that proves the change.

Everything else is discovery. If I can’t surprise myself, I probably can’t surprise you.
